Doctors and nurses spend a lot of time re-reading case notes to catch what went wrong. MAKI pulls entities from those narratives and labels them (symptoms, meds, treatment, physical exam) so review is faster and more consistent. Next.js front end; Python backend combines OpenAI's GPT-3 for open-ended extraction with `en_core_sci_lg`, a pre-trained spaCy biomedical model, for named-entity recognition. Speech-to-text and text-to-speech baked in for accessibility.

Team project (5 devs). We wanted to eliminate the "is this a carry-on?" anxiety at the gate. React Native hybrid app front-end, Django backend, and a bag-scanning service that used OpenCV, Scipy, and Imutils to classify luggage by dimensions from a phone camera.

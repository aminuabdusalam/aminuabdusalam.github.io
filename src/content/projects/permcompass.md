Employer-sponsored green cards are opaque, slow, and easy to lose track of. PermCompass compresses the whole journey (PERM Assessment, PWD, Labor Market Test, PERM filing, I-140, Priority Date wait, I-485 or Consular Processing, green card issued) into 11 short chapters with quizzes, plus a tracker view that takes your country of chargeability, EB category, and current phase and generates a full timeline of estimated dates all the way through. Special handling for the priority date wait, since India and China face very different realities than the rest of the world. Vanilla JS, installable, offline-capable, sage-cream theme matching the rest of the site.

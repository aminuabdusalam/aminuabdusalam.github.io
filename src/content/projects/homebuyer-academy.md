Buying a home is a huge financial decision, but the info is scattered across dense articles, YouTube explainers, and jargon-heavy government sites. HomeBuyer Academy compresses it into ten short chapters covering everything from credit scores and DTI to closing day and post-purchase upkeep. Each chapter ends with a quiz; correct answers earn XP and level you up from Dreamer onward. Built as a single-page PWA in vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with a service worker for offline access and `localStorage` for progress, so it installs on any phone or desktop with no accounts and no backend.

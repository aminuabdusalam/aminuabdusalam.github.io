Every developer's website starts with a "hello world" post. Here's mine.

## Why this exists

I've spent the last few years shipping commerce products at Xbox and quietly filling notebooks with half-thoughts about product, engineering, and how the two rub against each other. This site is my attempt to publish some of them.

Expect a mix:

- **Product notes** — what I'm learning from shipping subscription commerce to hundreds of millions of players.
- **Engineering ramblings** — the ex-engineer in me still cares about how things actually work.
- **Life stuff** — occasionally.

## What's on here

- **Projects** — things I've built, dating back to college.
- **About** — the elevator version of my story.
- **Writing** — this section. Posts, notes, and the occasional rant.

No newsletter, no popups, no cookie banner. Just a place to think out loud.
